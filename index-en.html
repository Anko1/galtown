OUR PRODUCTION

Combustive-lubricating materials - synthetic and mineral oil products, which include various types of fuel and lubricants: fuel (gasoline, diesel fuel, liquefied natural gas, liquefied petroleum gases), lubricants - (motor, transmission and special oils, mineral oils, plastic lubricants), oil-based fluids and special fluids (brake and cooling)

Our company on a regular basis implements fuel and is an importer of fuels and lubricants
It is possible to deliver fuel throughout the territory of Ukraine with gasoline rates from its own tank farms
The quality of the fuel is confirmed by the certificate of conformity and the product passport and is guaranteed
The price is discussed on the day of sale of fuel
Discounts are given to regular customers
Payment for fuel is made upon delivery or prepayment of 100%

Having a powerful and mobile service we provide warranty and post-warranty service, the purchased products are delivered to the buyer in the farm and launched by our customer service

Delivery of petroleum products and related products to filling stations in specialized stores is carried out by road. Their reception is carried out by the business entity only in the presence of accompanying documents (a commodity-transport waybill, an invoice, a receipt note, documents confirming the proper quality and safety of goods)

ecology

All products comply with environmental safety requirements, and the documentation includes an environmental impact assessment section. At least twice a year in shops selling fuel and lubricants, cleaning of storage tanks of fuel with utilization of residues in specially sealed containers is carried out with a view to their subsequent delivery for processing in accordance with the established procedure
